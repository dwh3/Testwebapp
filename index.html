While a senior at Ohio State, I took an Operations Research course which sparked my interest in the generalizable tools OR and Economics offer.

Professional Experience

Lockheed Martin

After graduation, I embarked on a professional journey at Lockheed Martin, gaining invaluable experience in the field. During my time at Lockheed Martin, I developed internal applications to be used on the manufacturing floor. The first generated process times across the site, automated the reporting of this to area managers, and displayed the results on a screen in the assembly area. The second used HTML, and SQL to make the kitting process more efficient, while allowing for the tracking of parts and orders throughout the warehouse.
